## Karl Daubmann

Karl Daubmann, FAIA, FAAR, is Provost and Professor at LTU. His teaching areas include design, digital design, robotic fabrication, construction, leadership, and multidisciplinary design. Daubmann taught at the University of Michigan, where he served as Associate Dean. Daubmann received his BArch from Roger Williams University and a SMArchS from MIT, where his concentration was in design computing.
