Christopher Westerlund is a computational designer and maker, and an alumnus of LTU’s College of Architecture and Design. He directs makeLab and chairs the planning committee for Co.de.D, Detroit AIA’s computational design community. Christopher is a designer in the computational design tech team at Stantec and previously worked with TDG Architects.
