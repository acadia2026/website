## Teng Teng

Teng Teng is a PhD Candidate and research fellow at the Polyhedral Structures Laboratory, Weitzman School of Design, UPenn. His research interests reside in mechatronics, robotics, advanced manufacturing, and materials. He holds two MS degrees from Cornell University and the University of Washington. He is particularly interested in addressing fabrication issues for design materialization, providing intelligent systems and computational software for designers and makers.
