## Azubike Ononye
Azubike Ononye (aka Zubi) is the Co-founder of Vessll, a consultancy focused on enabling AEC teams to transform their expertise into scalable digital solutions. Prior to that he was the Director of Digital technology at Inform Studio where he led a team responsible for shaping the firm’s innovation culture and digitally transforming the firm’s design and project management processes. He is also an adjunct professor educating the next generation of design professionals at Lawrence Technological University, Michigan.
