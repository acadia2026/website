## Carla Diana

Carla Diana is a designer, artist, author, and educator who explores the impact of future technologies through hands-on experiments in product design and tangible interaction. As a recipient of the Knight Foundation award, she curated Mobility Town at NewLab at Michigan Central. Carla holds an M.F.A. in 3D Design from Cranbrook Academy of Art and a B.E. in Mechanical Engineering from the Cooper Union.
