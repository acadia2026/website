## Lee Su Huang

Lee-Su Huang is an Associate Professor of Architecture at LTU CoAD and co-founder of SHO Architecture. His design research specializes in digital fabrication, parametric design, kinetic architecture, and interactive installations. Lee-Su received his B.Arch from Feng-Chia University and his M.Arch from Harvard GSD. He is co-organizer of the annual Playing Models Symposium on AI, Architecture, and Storytelling, the 4th iteration most recently held at Carnegie Mellon University on August 13-14, 2025.
