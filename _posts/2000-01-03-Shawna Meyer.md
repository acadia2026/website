## Shawna Meyer 
Lecturer, UM
smeyer@miami.edu

Shawna Meyer is a founding principal of Atelier Mey architecture practice based in Miami, FL and a lecturer at the University of Miami School of Architecture. The work of Atelier Mey acknowledges an obligation to confront the blase attitude towards architectural decision making, responsible for shaping the natural and built environments. Her work in practice, teaching and research exists at the confluence of social, cultural, economic and environmental forces. In 2022, Shawna was inducted into the John G. Williams Fellowship at the Fay Jones School of Architecture + Design.
